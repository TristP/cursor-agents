2 Product & packaging categories

Packaging & Paper EMPAP

Do you send products to French customers in packaging that you choose or control (boxes, envelopes, filler, labels, etc.)?

Example: you run an online shop and choose your own shipping boxes and envelopes.

Electrical & Electronic Equipment WEEE

Do you sell electrical or electronic products to French customers (devices that use electricity or batteries)?

Example: smartphones, laptops, kitchen appliances, LED lights, cables.

Batteries BAT

Do your products include standalone batteries or battery-powered devices where you are considered the producer?

Example: power banks, rechargeable batteries, toys with batteries included.

Textiles TLC

Do you sell clothing, shoes, household textiles or similar textile products to French customers?

Example: t-shirts, jeans, sneakers, bed linens, towels, curtains.

Furniture EA

Do you sell furniture (home, office, etc.) to French customers?

Example: chairs, tables, sofas, shelves, mattresses, outdoor furniture.
