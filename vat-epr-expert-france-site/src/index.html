Our Services

Two specialised services, one French compliance partner

Whether you need VAT representation, EPR representation, or both, we provide expert French compliance support tailored to e-commerce businesses and their advisors.

Fiscal representation for VAT in France

We act as your French fiscal representative for VAT purposes, handling all interactions with the tax authorities so you can operate in France with confidence.

  • French VAT registration and fiscal representation
  • Preparation and filing of periodic VAT returns (CA3)
  • Dedicated references for VAT payments and reconciliations
  • Ongoing communication with the French tax authorities
Learn more

Authorised representative for EPR in France

We serve as your authorised representative with French eco-organisations, managing your Extended Producer Responsibility obligations from registration to declarations.

  • Analysis of your French EPR obligations
  • Registration with relevant eco-organisations
  • UIN (unique identification number) management
  • Preparation and filing of eco-contribution declarations

How It Works

A clear path to French compliance

Our structured approach ensures smooth onboarding and ongoing compliance, whether you are a partner or an individual seller.

1

Assessment & onboarding

We analyse your situation, identify your French VAT and/or EPR obligations, and gather the necessary information using our structured templates.

2

Registration & set-up

We handle VAT and/or EPR registrations, obtain your UINs where applicable, and set up payment references and reporting structures.

3

Ongoing compliance

We prepare and file periodic VAT returns and EPR declarations, coordinate payments, and communicate with authorities and eco-organisations on your behalf.

For partners, we adapt this framework to your existing workflows and can provide customised onboarding, reporting and communication processes.
